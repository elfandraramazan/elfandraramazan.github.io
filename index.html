My All-Time Favorite Movies

A curated list of movies that have left a lasting impression on me.

Toy Story (1995)

A cowboy toy named Woody is replaced as his owner's favorite by a new spaceman figure, Buzz Lightyear, but they must work together to get back home after getting lost.

Toy Story 2 (1999)

Sheriff Woody is kidnapped by a greedy toy collector, forcing Buzz Lightyear and his friends to launch a daring rescue mission to save him.

Toy Story 3 (2010)

As Andy prepares for college, Woody convinces his accidentally donated toy friends to escape a prison-like daycare and return home before their owner leaves.

Toy Story 4 (2019)

Woody embarks on a journey of self-discovery while helping Forky find his place in the world.
